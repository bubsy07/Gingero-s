Gingero's

Inspired from the southern Amalfi coast of Italy, and a unique Welsh upbringing Gingero is proud to serve you with his special blend of rich roasted arabica and robusta coffee for that special welsh/Italian taste experience. We also pride ourselves on our unique recipe Dinky Donuts and creamy soft flavour burst ice cream, Danish pastries, and delicious cakes, Don't take our word for it come and try for yourselves
